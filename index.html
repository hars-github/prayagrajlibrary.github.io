Service with Excellenc

The library values excellence, individually and collectively. We offer quality library services to all readers with positive attitude. We strive to earn the trust and confidence of all readers. Library staffs are professional and always willing to understand reader’s requirements. We demonstrate friendliness, reliability and proactiveness to offering timely services to readers.

Core Values

Lifelong Learning

We play an important role in society so that study and learning can thrive. The library supports reading and lifelong learning in society without any discrimination.

Freedom of Know

We respect every users right to know and right to read. Treating everyone with dignity, respect, courtesy and compassion.

Staff Appreciation

We recognize that the library employees are our most valuable resource. We value team spirit and treat each other with respect. We always encourage staff to pursue their personal and professional goals.

Integrity and Trust

We provide quality library services without bias with integrity. Our employees operate the library system efficiently and effectively.
